Lasagna

Lasagna is a very popular dish from Italy

It is made from pasta, tomato sauce, different kinds of cheese's and some species

Ingridients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mosarella Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Put the pasta inside the baking plate
  2. Put one layer of tomato paste
  3. Put the cooked Ground Beef
  4. Put the Mosarella Cheese
  5. Put some spices on top
  6. Repeat the process until you reache the top of the plate
  7. Bake on the onven at 160F degrees for 30 minutes
